Your Trusted Partner for Car Rentals

At Top Fleet, we believe that getting from point A to point B should be more than just a journey; it should be an experience. Founded in 2010, we set out with a mission to deliver top-notch car rental services tailored to the diverse needs of our customers.

  • Vision: To redefine car rentals by combining affordability, convenience, and premium service.
  • Fleet: Boasting a wide array of vehicles, from fuel-efficient economy cars to luxurious rides, we ensure there’s a perfect match for every occasion.
  • Commitment: Sustainability is at the heart of what we do. Our eco-friendly initiatives include hybrid and electric vehicle options to reduce our carbon footprint.

Our Story

Top Fleet started as a small family-owned business with a handful of cars and a big dream: to make car rentals hassle-free and enjoyable for everyone. Over the years, we’ve grown into a trusted name in the industry, serving customers across multiple locations with the same personalized touch that set us apart from the start. Our commitment to innovation has led us to adopt cutting-edge technology, ensuring a seamless experience from booking to returning your vehicle.

  • Team: Our dedicated professionals bring decades of expertise, ensuring that every aspect of your journey is handled with care.
  • Reach, Local Feel: While we’ve expanded to serve a global audience, we maintain the friendly, reliable service that has always been our hallmark.
